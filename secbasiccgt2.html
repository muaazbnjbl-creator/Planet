Diet of the Strongest Forest Animals

Discover what fuels the mightiest creatures of the forest. From herbivores to apex predators, here’s their menu in the wild.

African Forest Elephant

Loxodonta cyclotis
Diet:
  • Leaves
  • Fruits
  • Bark & roots

Grizzly Bear

Ursus arctos
Diet:
  • Fish (salmon)
  • Berries
  • Roots & plants
  • Occasional mammals

Tiger

Panthera tigris
Diet:
  • Deer
  • Wild boar
  • Buffalo

Gorilla

Gorilla spp.
Diet:
  • Leaves
  • Fruits
  • Bamboo shoots

Jaguar

Panthera onca
Diet:
  • Capybara
  • Caiman
  • Turtles
  • Deer

Leopard

Panthera pardus
Diet:
  • Antelope
  • Monkeys
  • Birds
  • Rodents

Orangutan

Pongo spp.
Diet:
  • Fruits (figs, durians)
  • Leaves
  • Bark
  • Insects occasionally

Wild Boar

Sus scrofa
Diet:
  • Roots
  • Fruits
  • Insects
  • Small animals

Moose

Alces alces
Diet:
  • Aquatic plants
  • Leaves
  • Shrubs

Green Anaconda

Eunectes murinus
Diet:
  • Fish
  • Birds
  • Mammals (capybara, deer)
  • Caiman
